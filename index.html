What is ADSTOCALL?

ADSTOCALL is the ultimate platform for passive income for mobile users. It’s an interactive platform where users, advertisers, and mobile network providers can engage with one another in a single ecosystem. It’s an interactive digital ad platform that acts as a passive income source for users. The main objective is to make the digital ad experience more seamless, profitable, and accessible for all.

How ADSTOCALL Works?

The overall process is simple and straightforward. ADSTOCALL offers 02 (two) convenient options for advertisers:

  • Auditions
  • Digital billboards

Auditions can be best described as a "caller ad", where advertisers can promote their market offerings. These Auditions will be set up with the contact numbers of our registered users. An advertiser can also publish their ads on digital billboards published on the homepage of ADSTOCALL. On the other hand, users can subscribe to ads and their contact numbers will be used to broadcast the auditions according to their subscriptions. This leaves us with the mobile network operator who can collaborate with ADSTOCALL to connect users and advertisers. Both the users and mobile network operators will receive a percentage for each audition received from the advertiser.

Benefits of ADSTOCALL

The benefits of using ADSTOCALL are as follows: For advertisers: A quick and easy way to advertise a brand For users: A passive income generator For mobile network operators: Revenue generation with existing operator features

Network Providers/Mobile Network Operators



Network providers, or mobile network operators, are a crucial part of the Audition process in ADSTOCALL. Network providers can use "caller ads" to promote an advertiser’s offering through Auditions and generate revenues.
